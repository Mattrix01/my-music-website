About Me

I am an experienced producer and mixing engineer, with over 11 years of experience in the industry. I have had the privilege of working on projects all over London, at numerous venues including Abbey Road and Metropolis.

I have worked with hundreds of clients from across the globe, including X Factor star Ryan Lawrie, The Voice finalists and winners Clara Hurtado and Jessica Hammond, Britain’s Got Talent’s Henrietta Adele, Notting Hill Music songwriter Joe Killington and International music icon Rahat Fateh Ali Kahn, as well as having a Number 1 in the iTunes World Album Charts with IM1313 - to name a few.

I have produced, mixed and mastered everything from modern electronic and urban music to film and live recorded sound tracks to visual art or animation, which has had radio play as well as chart presence. My experience gets my clients music where it needs to be. My versatile, innovative and proactive approach to all my projects, makes me a great choice for your music production and engineering needs.

Please feel free to listen to my showreel which features tracks I have both produced and Mixed/Mastered in the past few years.
